# Statement of Work — Acme Pilot Account (Baseline v1.0)
[SYNTHETIC / FICTIONAL — for pipeline testing only]

## Section 4: Support & Maintenance

**4.1 Support Hours**
Supplier shall provide L2 support during business hours (09:00-18:00, Monday-Friday, local time).

**4.2 Monitoring**
Supplier shall monitor production systems using standard monitoring tools during business hours.

**4.3 Backup**
Daily backups are taken and retained for 30 days.

## Section 6: Governance

**6.1 Reporting**
A monthly service report is provided to the client.

**6.2 Escalation**
Issues are escalated per the standard L1 -> L2 -> L3 escalation model.
